Three Calligraphy Logo Concepts: Rebranding DIILULU

We explored three distinct calligraphy-inspired redesigns for DIILULU. Each captures a different aspect of what this blog represents: the intersection of AI creation and human creativity.

Option 1: Flourish Manuscript

Copperplate italic script with dramatic flourishes. Victorian elegance meets contemporary AI blog. Deep indigo and rose gold palette. Strong for literary audiences but risks looking dated.

Option 2: Geometric Brush Stroke (Recommended)

Modern brush calligraphy meets geometric precision. Thick angle cuts, broken letterforms with visible stroke layering. Matte black or teal with electric accents. This one wins: it visually represents human brushstrokes + algorithmic structure. Scales perfectly across all platforms.

Option 3: Liquid Serif Hybrid

Serif geometry with liquid, watercolor-style brushstroke terminals. Forest green or burgundy with soft gold accents. Refined but subtle—doesn't punch through crowded social feeds.

Verdict: Geometric Brush Stroke captures DIILULU's true identity: neither pure tech nor pure art, but both. It speaks to forward-thinking creatives who want algorithmic amplification of their writing.
